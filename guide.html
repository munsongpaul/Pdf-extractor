Why Extract Text from PDF Files?

PDFs are great for sharing documents that look the same on every device, but they are notoriously difficult to edit. Extracting text allows you to repurpose content for reports, data analysis, or simply to copy information quickly.

Best Practices

When extracting text, ensure your PDF is not an image-based scan (like a photo of a document). Our tool works best with documents that contain selectable text. If you have an image, you may need an OCR (Optical Character Recognition) tool first.
