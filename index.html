Frequently Asked Questions

Is this online PDF text extractor safe to use?

Yes, absolutely. Unlike conventional cloud-based converters that force you to upload documents to external servers—exposing your sensitive data—our tool operates entirely client-side. By leveraging advanced local JavaScript APIs, your files are read entirely on your own device. Your private documents are never uploaded, tracked, or saved anywhere.

Can I extract text from secured or restricted PDFs?

If a PDF document requires an open-password to read, you must unlock it before processing. However, if the file is simply locked with print or copy restrictions, our client-side utility safely bypasses those restrictive browser policies to grab the underlying text elements directly.

Why choose a client-side extractor over cloud alternatives?

Cloud utilities are often limited by internet connection speeds, throttle your maximum allowed file sizes, or put your private corporate records at risk of data leaks. Processing your assets locally guarantees immediate script output execution regardless of file size, complete processing offline, and total data privacy.

Does this tool support scanned image PDFs (OCR)?

Currently, this utility extracts native font text layers embedded inside digital PDF documents. If your PDF consists purely of photos or flattened images from a scanner without a digital text layer, the tool will not find character data to copy. We recommend using native text documents for best results.
